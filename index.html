transdermal estradiol gel

This is good if you're in a pinch or don't feel like gathering so many ingredients at once like other recipes require. All you need is a miligram scale, some weighing paper (you can simply cut a piece of printer paper into sixteenths), some estradiol powder, and ethanol-based hand sanitizer.

Ingredients

  1. 73 grams of ethanol-based hand sanitizer
  2. 0.18 grams of estradiol powder
  3. (optional) orange essential oil

Procedure

again, you'll need a miligram scale and weighing paper for this. to store this, use an airless pump bottle.

  1. weigh out 73 grams of ethanol-based hand sanitizer, put that in your pump bottle
  2. weigh out 0.18 grams of estradiol powder, put that in your pump bottle
  3. put in a few mililiters of orange essential oil, this is good for scent and it is a penetration enhancer
  4. shake shake shake and let it sit for an hour so the estradiol can dissolve.

Transdermal Progesterone Gel

this is experimental. there isn't much information out there on alcohol-based transdermal progesterone gel, so i took a leap of faith and made my own recipe.

here's what you should know:

  • alcohol-based transdermal progesterone gives higher peak blood serum levels than cream-based transdermal progesterone, but it dips faster.
  • you 100% should be applying this gel to an area of high absorption, such as the scrotum, penis, armpit, or inner thigh.
  • otherwise, this is the same procedure as making transdermal estradiol gel. you need weighing paper, a miligram scale, and an airless pump bottle.

nonetheless, here is the recipe:

ingredients

  • 69 grams of ethanol-based hand sanitizer
  • 4.15 grams of orange-oil
  • 2.25 grams of progesterone powder (DO NOT USE SYNTHETIC.)

procedure

  1. weigh out 69 grams of hand sanitizer, put it in your bottle.
  2. weigh out 4.15 grams of orange oil, put it in your bottle
  3. weigh out 2.25 grams of progesterone powder, put it in your bottle
  4. shake shake shake! this will take a while to fully dissolve in the hand sanitizer, since it's a high concentration.
