g-anthro

This is a more recent counterpart of Freiburg's anthropometric measurements, which, although it has a large amount of data across male and female sexes, it's rather outdated (the data were recorded in 1988); this prompted the development of this tool, which has data from 1987 women and 4083 men. One may use this to better visually conceptualize how their measurements compare to other people.
